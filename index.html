Washington County Economic Indicators


June 2017
Hello all,

Washington County continued to set records with another historically low unemployment rate in May 2017 - 3.0 percent. Some cities in Washington County saw unemployment rates below 3 percent in April, not seasonally adjusted.

Total employment grew by 1.7 percent over the year (+4,800 jobs). Transportation and warehousing and construction led the charge with year over year employment growth of 25 percent and 7.4 percent respectively. Manufacturing maintained current employment levels, while retail continued to lose jobs over the year.
